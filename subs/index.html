How It Works
1
Browse
Send /services to the SUBS bot or browse this page. Each service lists features, price, and period.
2
Subscribe
Send PFT to the SUBS protocol address with memo subs.subscribe and the service ID. One transaction.
3
Access
The service verifies your subscription on-chain. No passwords, no API keys. Your payment IS your credential.
4
Cancel
Stop paying. When your period expires, access ends. No forms, no emails, no retention dark patterns.
