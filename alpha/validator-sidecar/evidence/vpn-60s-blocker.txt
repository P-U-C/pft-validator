Root cause: a scoring pass needs ~6 min of wall clock (H100 generates 15,981 completion tokens at ~54 tok/s).
Modal holds the connection and issues a 303 to a result-polling URL at ~150s, which the sidecar's

90s before that redirect can ever fire. The sidecar reports this as RUNTIME_UNAVAILABLE.
