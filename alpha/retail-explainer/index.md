# Post Fiat Explained

*What it is, how it works, and why it matters — in plain language.*

---

## The One-Paragraph Version

Post Fiat is its own blockchain — an independent Layer 1 network forked from XRP's codebase but running separately with its own validators, its own token (PFT), and its own purpose. Where most crypto networks reward you for holding tokens or running infrastructure, Post Fiat rewards you for doing real work. You submit tasks, an AI evaluates the quality, and you earn PFT based on how useful your contribution actually was. The best contributions feed a collective intelligence system called the Data Lake, which generates real trading profits. Those profits create organic demand for PFT. The token goes up because the network got smarter — not because more people bought in.

---

## How It Actually Works

### The Task Node

The Task Node is where work happens on Post Fiat. You open it, describe what you want to work on, and the AI generates a task matched to your skills and the network's needs. It reads your history on the chain, understands your context, and routes you to work where your contribution matters most.

This is not a bounty board. You do not scroll through a list of tasks and pick one. The system generates personalized work proposals based on what you bring to the table. A trader gets trading research questions. A developer gets infrastructure tasks. A researcher gets analysis briefs. The matching is the product.

Once you complete the work, you publish the evidence and submit it for review. The review includes an AI novelty check — has this been done before? — a sybil similarity score, and a human verification question that tests whether you actually did the work. If you pass, you earn PFT. If the work is exceptional, you earn a multiplier — sometimes double the listed reward.

### Portable Pseudonymous Reputation

Your reputation on Post Fiat is attached to your wallet address, not your name. Every task you complete, every review you pass, every reward you earn is recorded on-chain. This creates a portable, verifiable track record that you own.

This matters because it is yours. You do not need a platform's permission to prove your work history. If Post Fiat disappeared tomorrow, your contributions would still be on the ledger. Any future system that reads the chain can see what you built, how it was scored, and what you earned. That is not true of any centralized work platform.

The pseudonymity is also important. You can build a reputation without doxxing yourself. The chain knows your wallet produced high-quality intelligence. It does not need to know your name.

### AI-Mediated Work Routing

Most platforms match workers to tasks using keyword filters or manual curation. Post Fiat uses AI to read your full contribution history and generate tasks that fit your specific capabilities.

The routing gets better over time. The more you contribute, the more context the AI has, and the more precisely it matches you to high-value work. Contributors who have been on the network for weeks report that their generated tasks feel personally calibrated — because they are.

This is different from a recommendation algorithm showing you content. The AI is not guessing what you want to see. It is designing work that the network needs and that your skills can deliver. The overlap between those two things is where the value is.

### Validator Selection

Post Fiat runs on its own set of validators — not XRP's validators, not Ethereum's. The validator set is selected using a combination of institutional reputation, uptime metrics, and AI-scored credibility assessment.

Validators do not earn ongoing token rewards. There are no staking incentives. Validators run because they use the network, not because they are paid to validate. This eliminates the perverse incentives that plague proof-of-stake systems: no one is running a validator just to collect rewards. They run one because they have a genuine stake in the network functioning correctly.

The infrastructure cost is minimal — a few hundred dollars per year for a server. The barrier to validation is not capital. It is alignment.

### Market Intelligence and the Data Lake

Every verified contribution on Post Fiat feeds the Data Lake — a growing repository of human-originated intelligence that no language model can replicate from public training data. This intelligence includes task outputs, research analyses, operational observations, and verified work products.

The Data Lake is the network's structural advantage. AI models can synthesize public information. They cannot synthesize the operating context of someone who just built a chain indexer, discovered a 733-bot wash trading network, or mapped where crypto signal confidence scoring inverts. That context is non-replicable. It compounds with every new contribution.

Trading agents consume the Data Lake to generate real market positions. The profits from those positions create organic buy pressure on PFT. This is the flywheel: better intelligence produces better trades, better trades create token demand, token demand rewards the contributors who produced the intelligence.

### PFT Rewards

PFT has a fixed supply of 100 billion tokens with no ongoing issuance. You earn PFT by completing verified tasks on the Task Node. Reward amounts range from about 1,000 PFT to 10,000+ PFT per task, with multipliers for exceptional quality.

The reward system is quality-weighted, not volume-weighted. Ten mediocre submissions earn less than one deep, original contribution. The review system actively distinguishes between work that an AI could have generated from public data and work that contains genuine human insight. The gap between those two things is what the network pays for.

Daily airdrops recognize the most active contributors based on reputation. These are separate from task rewards — they are recognition payments for sustained, high-quality participation.

## What Post Fiat Is Not

**Post Fiat is not an XRP token.** PFT runs on its own blockchain with network ID 2025. It has its own validators, its own consensus, and its own token. The codebase originated from an XRP fork, but the networks are entirely separate. You cannot send PFT on the XRP ledger or use XRP wallets with PFT.

**Post Fiat is not a gig platform.** You do not browse a list of bounties. The AI generates personalized work matched to your capabilities. The value is in the routing, not the listing.

**Post Fiat is not a staking protocol.** There are no staking rewards, no liquidity mining, no yield farming. PFT is earned by doing work, not by locking tokens.

**Post Fiat is not an AI chatbot.** The AI is the coordination layer, not the product. It evaluates work, routes tasks, and maintains quality. The product is the human intelligence the network produces.

**Post Fiat is not a speculative token with no utility.** PFT has six active demand drivers: wallet activation, transaction fees, agent micropayments, signal marketplace, task rewards, and trading profits from the Data Lake.

**Post Fiat is not a closed system.** Anyone can join. There is no application process, no waitlist, no token gate. You get a wallet, fund it with PFT, and start contributing. The system selects for quality through its review process, not through access restrictions.

## FAQ

**How do I start?**
Get a wallet at [tasknode.postfiat.org](https://tasknode.postfiat.org). Acquire PFT. Open the Task Node and describe what you want to work on. The system generates a task for you.

**How much can I earn?**
Task rewards range from ~1,000 to 10,000+ PFT. Quality multipliers can double the listed reward. Top contributors earn 100,000-300,000 PFT per week through consistent, high-quality work.

**What kind of work is there?**
Research and analysis, software development, infrastructure building, network governance, product design, content creation, and review/verification. The Task Node matches work to your skills.

**Is my data safe?**
All task communication is encrypted using keystone protocol. Your identity is pseudonymous — attached to your wallet, not your name. Your contributions are on-chain and verifiable, but your personal information is not.

**Where can I see the network?**
[pft.permanentupperclass.com/lens/](https://pft.permanentupperclass.com/lens/) — live network graph, contributor activity, sybil detection, airdrop tracking.

**Where can I read the daily intelligence?**

**Is Post Fiat built on XRP?**
No. Post Fiat forked XRP's codebase in the same way Bitcoin Cash forked Bitcoin. They share code ancestry but are completely independent networks. PFT does not exist on the XRP ledger.
